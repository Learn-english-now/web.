What is Grammar?

Grammar is the set of rules that govern how words are combined to form sentences in a language. It ensures clarity and precision in communication.

Why is Grammar Important?

  • Improves writing and speaking accuracy.
  • Helps avoid misunderstandings.
  • Essential for academic and professional success.
