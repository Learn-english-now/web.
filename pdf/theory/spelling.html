What is spelling?

Spelling is the correct way of writing words using the letters of the alphabet. It ensures that the meaning of written language is clear and understandable.

Why is spelling important in English?

  • Helps avoid confusion in writing
  • Improves reading and writing skills
  • Enhances academic and professional communication
