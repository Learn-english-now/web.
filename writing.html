🧠 Advanced Vocabulary (C1)

Learn and apply these words in real context:

  • Ephemeral
  • Ubiquitous
  • Conundrum
  • Disenfranchise
  • Ostentatious
  • Ephemeral: Short-lived, temporary.
  • Ubiquitous: Present everywhere.
  • Conundrum: A difficult problem or puzzle.
  • Disenfranchise: Remove rights or power.
  • Ostentatious: Overly showy, trying to impress.

📖 Reading Comprehension

Read the text and answer each question.

In today’s world, digital presence is almost ubiquitous. Social media platforms and messaging apps are used by nearly everyone, everywhere. To maintain privacy in this constant exposure, many people—especially younger users—prefer ephemeral content like disappearing stories or messages. However, this gives rise to a conundrum: if these moments vanish quickly, do they truly disappear, or do they leave digital traces that affect us anyway? The challenge lies in understanding the consequences of what we share, and how it shapes the way others see us.
