What will you learn?

Levels A1 & A2

At levels A1 and A2, you will begin your English journey by developing the four essential skills: Reading, Writing, Speaking, and Listening. Through dynamic and interactive activities, you will learn basic vocabulary, simple grammar structures, and useful phrases to communicate in everyday situations in a fun and effective way.

Levels B1 & B2

At levels B1 and B2, you will improve your fluency and comprehension. You’ll work with more complex texts, enhance your writing, and participate in more natural conversations. You’ll also practice expressing opinions, giving detailed descriptions, and understanding different types of audio and video. These levels prepare you to use English in work, travel, and academic contexts.

Level C1

At level C1, you will reach an advanced command of English. You’ll explore idiomatic expressions, formal and informal language, academic texts, and professional writing. You’ll also develop skills to debate, clearly present ideas, and understand a variety of accents. This level allows you to communicate confidently in any personal or professional setting.
