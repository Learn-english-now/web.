A Day at the Beach

Last Saturday, my family and I went to the beach. We arrived early in the morning and the weather was sunny and warm. My brother swam in the sea and I played with the sand. We had lunch under a big umbrella. After lunch, we took a short walk along the shore and collected some seashells. My parents relaxed while reading their books. In the afternoon, we all ate ice cream. It was a perfect day, and we had fun all the time.
