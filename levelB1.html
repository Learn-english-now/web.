A Journey to Remember

Last summer, Emma and her best friend Lucas decided to go on a trip across Europe. It was their first time traveling without their families, so they were both excited and nervous.

They started their journey in France, where they visited the Eiffel Tower and ate croissants every morning. Then, they traveled by train to Italy and explored Rome’s historical sites, like the Colosseum and the Vatican. Emma loved taking pictures, and Lucas enjoyed trying different types of pasta in every city.

One day in Venice, they got lost in the narrow streets. Instead of panicking, they decided to enjoy the moment. They discovered a small café where they had the best hot chocolate of their lives. Sometimes, getting lost can lead to the best experiences.

Their last stop was Germany, where they stayed with a local family they met through a travel app. The family was kind and showed them around Munich. Emma and Lucas practiced their German, played with the family’s dog, and learned how to cook traditional German food.

After two weeks, they returned home tired but happy. The trip taught them not only about new cultures but also about independence and friendship. It was truly a journey to remember.

  • Trip – viaje
  • Historical sites – sitios históricos
  • Get lost – perderse
  • Enjoy the moment – disfrutar el momento
  • Local family – familia local
  • Friendship – amistad
  • Independence – independencia

1. Where did Emma and Lucas start their trip?

2. What did Emma and Lucas enjoy doing in Italy?

3. What happened in Venice?

4. Who hosted them in Germany?

5. What did they learn during the trip?
