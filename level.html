My Family

Hello! My name is Emma. I am 10 years old. I have one brother and one sister. My mother is a teacher. My father is a doctor. We live in a big house. I love my family!
Vocabulary ▶
  • Brother – hermano
  • Sister – hermana
  • Mother – madre
  • Father – padre
  • Big house – casa grande

Comprehension Questions

1. How old is Emma?

2. What is Emma’s mother’s job?

3. Where does Emma live?

🎉 Congratulations! All answers are correct! 🎉
